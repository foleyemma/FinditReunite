Step-By-Step Process

  • enter lost or found items details (e.g., item lost or found, category, photos, date, time, etc)

  • review details entered and submit the lost or found item report.

  • receive email with your user dashboard login and account information.

  • within the dashboard you can print fliers or post a free Ad of the submitted lost or found item.

  • receive notifications when newly submitted items match the details on your entry.
